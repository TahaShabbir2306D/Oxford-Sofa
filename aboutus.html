Our Story

Founded in 2000 , Oxford Sofas emerged from a passion for quality craftsmanship and a deep appreciation for stylish, functional design. What started as a small venture dedicated to creating sofas that blend seamlessly into any living space has evolved into a brand known for its commitment to excellence.
